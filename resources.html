If you are experiencing acute psychological pain or are at risk of suicide — seek help immediately. Crisis hotlines operate 24/7.

Emergency Help (USA)

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

988

Free, confidential, 24/7 support for people in distress. Call or text 988.

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741

Free, 24/7 text-based crisis support across the US.

Emergency Services

911

For life-threatening emergencies. Call 911 for immediate assistance.

National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI)

1-800-950-NAMI (6264)

Mental health information, support, and referrals. Mon–Fri, 10am–10pm ET.

SAMHSA National Helpline

1-800-662-4357

Free, confidential treatment referral for mental health and substance use disorders. 24/7.

National Sexual Assault Hotline

1-800-656-HOPE (4673)

Confidential support for survivors of sexual assault. 24/7.
