About the Portal

The Psychodiagnostic Portal provides access to validated psychological testing instruments. All tests are freely available for self-assessment and educational purposes.

Test results are for informational purposes only and do not constitute a medical diagnosis. If significant symptoms are identified, consultation with a qualified professional is recommended.

Psychometric Properties

Instrument Authors Reliability (Cronbach α) Validity
DASS-21 Lovibond S.H., Lovibond P.F. (1995) α = 0.82–0.93 Convergent, discriminant, criterion
Irrational Procrastination Scale Steel P. (2010), adaptation α = 0.78–0.85 Construct, criterion

Operating Principles

Privacy

All data is processed locally in your browser. The server does not receive or store user responses. Results are visible only to you.

Scientific Rigor

Only instruments with confirmed psychometric properties are used, adapted and validated on English-speaking samples.

Informed Consent

Users acknowledge that self-test results do not replace professional consultation. The portal is not responsible for independent interpretation of results.
